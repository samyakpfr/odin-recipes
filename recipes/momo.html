Mo:Mo

Mo:Mo is a authentic Nepali dish, usually served with tomato sauce.

Chicken Mo:Mo Recipe:
Ingredients:

  • 1 cup minced chicken
  • 1 small onion (finely chopped)
  • 1 tsp garlic paste
  • 1 tsp ginger paste
  • 2 green chilies (finely chopped)
  • 2 tbsp chopped coriander leaves
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 tsp soy sauce
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • Water (for dough and sealing)
Instructions:
  1. Mix flour with water to make a soft dough. Cover and rest for 30 minutes.
  2. Combine chicken, onion, garlic, ginger, chilies, coriander, salt, and soy sauce to make the filling.
  3. Roll dough into small circles. Place filling in the center and fold into desired shapes.

  5. Serve hot with spicy momo chutney.
